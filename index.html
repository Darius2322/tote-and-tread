Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025. This policy explains how Tote & Tread Collection collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our app, we may collect: your full name, email address, phone number, and delivery address when you create an account or place an order. We also collect your profile picture if you choose to upload one.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to: process and fulfil your orders, send you order confirmations and updates, provide customer support, and improve our app experience. We do not use your data for advertising profiling or sell it to third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

All your data is stored securely on Supabase — a trusted, enterprise-grade database platform. We use Row Level Security (RLS) policies so that you can only access your own data. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text. All connections use HTTPS/TLS encryption.

4. Profile Pictures

If you upload a profile picture, it is stored in a secure Supabase Storage bucket with public read access (so your avatar can be displayed in the app). You can change or remove your profile picture at any time from your account settings.

5. Contact & Saved Addresses

When you place an order and opt to save your contact details, your phone number and delivery address are stored to make future checkouts faster. This data is only visible to you and admins. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us.

6. Notifications

We send in-app notifications about your orders, promotions, and system updates. You can view and dismiss these at any time from the notification centre.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to: access the personal data we hold about you, request correction of inaccurate data, request deletion of your account and associated data, and withdraw consent for data processing. To exercise these rights, contact us at [email protected].

8. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase (database & storage), Vercel (app hosting), and WhatsApp (order communication). Each of these has their own privacy policies. We share only the minimum data necessary with these services.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notification. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
