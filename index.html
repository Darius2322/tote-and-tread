About Tote & Tread

Discover the story behind Kenya's premier fashion destination

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Tote & Tread has grown from a small boutique in Nairobi to Kenya's premier fashion destination. Our name reflects our dual passion for exquisite bags ("Tote") and premium footwear ("Tread").

What began as a passion project has evolved into a brand that celebrates Kenyan craftsmanship while embracing global fashion standards. Each piece in our collection tells a story of dedication, quality, and timeless elegance.

Our Vision

To be Africa's leading fashion destination, setting trends and defining style for generations to come.

Our Mission

To provide premium quality bags and footwear that combine Kenyan craftsmanship with global fashion standards.

Our Motto

"Elegant Accessories, Timeless Style" - Quality that lasts, style that endures.

Our Values

Craftsmanship

Every piece is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, using only the finest materials.

Sustainability

Ethical sourcing and eco-friendly practices are at the heart of everything we do.

Community

We support local artisans and give back to the communities that inspire us.

Innovation

Constantly evolving to bring you the latest in fashion trends and technology.
